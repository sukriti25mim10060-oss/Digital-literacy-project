
# **Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students in India)**

✔ **Use strong, unique passwords**
Create passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using the same password across multiple accounts.

✔ **Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)**
Turn on 2FA for email, social media, and banking apps for extra security.

✔ **Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks**
Do not access banking or personal accounts on public Wi-Fi networks in cafes or colleges.

✔ **Do not click on suspicious links**
Be cautious of unknown emails, messages, or links, especially those creating urgency or offering rewards.

✔ **Keep your devices and apps updated**
Regular updates fix security vulnerabilities and protect your data.

✔ **Be careful while sharing personal information**
Avoid posting sensitive details like phone number, address, or college ID publicly.

✔ **UPI Safety: Never share OTP or UPI PIN**
Banks or apps will never ask for your OTP or PIN. Sharing it can lead to instant financial loss.

✔ **UPI Safety: Verify payment requests before approving**
Always check the sender’s name and purpose before approving any “Collect Request” on apps like Google Pay or PhonePe.

✔ **Download apps only from official stores**
Use Google Play Store or Apple App Store to avoid malicious apps.

✔ **Log out from shared devices**
Always log out from accounts when using public or shared computers.
